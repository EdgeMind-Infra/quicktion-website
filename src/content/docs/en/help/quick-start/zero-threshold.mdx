title: Is it really zero-threshold?
description: No background knowledge, no typing, full visibility — Quicktion makes AI a truly usable digital teammate.
---

### 01 As simple as chatting — no background knowledge required

Most software has a learning curve. You have to know a bit of something before you can use it.

We're different. Just tell the Agent, and it gets it done.

You don't even have to think through what to do — we'll suggest the next step, and you confirm or tweak.

— Not waiting for you to ask; we think ahead.

### 02 You can see exactly what the Agent is doing

Many people worry about AI: "I can't see what it's doing — it makes me nervous."

With us, every step the Agent takes is shown to you in real time.

— Which creators it's looking up, which data it's analyzing, what content it's generating. You can interrupt, redirect, or pause at any point.

Even better: when the Agent opens a page to operate, you can step in and operate it yourself.

— It's like hiring an assistant, but you stay in the driver's seat.

### 03 No typing fatigue — interact with AI visually

Traditional AI tools need typing or voice input. Our interaction is different: you click cards to drive the conversation.

- When creator-filter options come up, tap to pick from the list.
- For important decisions, one tap confirms.

— Fine-grained control, but we only ask when it matters.

### 04 Agents open any webpage and act like a real person

This is one of our core capabilities: inside a virtual machine, the Agent drives a browser exactly like a human — opening any platform and finishing work on your behalf.

Need to update business hours on Douyin Laike, or send a creator invitation on a platform? The AI logs in, finds the right function, and steps through it.

- Each task runs in its own VM — accounts are isolated, no interference.
- Multiple Agents can run in parallel without conflicting with your own logged-in sessions.
- Data stays encrypted end-to-end.

### 05 Tell it once, it remembers forever

A common AI pain: every new chat, you re-introduce yourself.

Your store name? Your main cuisine? Your marketing preferences? Our Agents remember all of it.

— Say it once, we keep it for good.

### 06 Beyond marketing — general-purpose abilities too

Alongside marketing Agents, the platform supports general-purpose AI as an all-around assistant:

- Chat with AI on anything.
- Generate images and videos.
- Produce documents in any format (Word, PDF, Markdown…).

:::tip
The point of technology isn't to make people learn more. It's to let people do less and get more.
:::
