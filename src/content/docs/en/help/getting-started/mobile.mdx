### 01 What's possible on mobile?

Once connected, what you can do in WeChat or Feishu is nearly identical to the desktop — no feature loss:

- **Issue tasks**: tell it what to do.
- **Check progress**: ask about an in-flight task anytime.
- **Receive results**: plans and reports arrive directly in chat.
- **Tweak**: not happy with the output? Say so in chat — the AI regenerates.
- **Approve**: for steps that need a decision, it waits for your word in WeChat.

All of this happens in the chat UI you already use — no app switching.

If you want to review the full task trace, log into the web console. Every Agent step is captured and visualized.

### 02 Who is this for?

- **Busy operators**: no time to sit at a computer, but never far from the phone.
- **Multi-store owners**: several locations, moving around, phone is convenient.
- **Instant-response types**: want notifications the moment a task finishes, without polling a dashboard.
- **Team collaboration**: owner and operations staff share a group chat; everyone sees the results.

### 03 Is my account safe?

You don't need to hand over any credentials — we don't store your communication data.

The AI only receives commands and delivers results. It cannot read your chat history or send messages on your behalf.

You can disconnect and revoke authorization anytime.

:::tip
AI's real value is letting you start tasks and receive results anywhere — not chaining you to a desk.
:::
