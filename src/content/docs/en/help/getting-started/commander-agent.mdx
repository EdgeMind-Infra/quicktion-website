Quicktion lets you direct AI from WeChat.

### Commander + specialist Agents

In WeChat, you talk to the Commander Agent: it understands your need, issues the commands, and lets the right specialist Agents do the actual work — diagnose a store, find creators, list products. The Commander then sends the results back to you.

Our platform integrates with WeChat, Feishu, DingTalk, QQ, and WeCom — the major Chinese messaging apps — so you can issue commands from your phone.

You're busy serving customers in the store. Your phone buzzes — the AI: "Creator marketing plan is ready, please review."

You're walking down the street and remember to update your opening hours. You send: "Change business hours to 10 AM–10 PM." The AI updates it in the background and replies confirming it's done.
