### Diagnose first, then execute

### 01 Store Diagnostic Agent — your "data doctor"

The Store Diagnostic Agent plugs into your store's first-party data and gives the store a regular check-up.

It scans sales performance, traffic structure, and user reviews across the board, and produces a "store health report" —

with actionable conclusions so you know exactly where to intervene.

### 02 Score Analysis Agent — your "missing-points detective"

Douyin Laike has a "operations score" system; the score directly controls how much traffic your store gets.

The Score Analysis Agent finds which modules are underperforming and how to recover points, then hands them off to the Operator Agents:

- Incomplete store info? The **Storefront Agent** fills it in.
- Not enough creator videos? The **Creator Matching Agent** finds them.
- Local ads not running? The **Local Ads Agent** runs them.

They act based on the real data; your job is just to confirm.

### Lifting the score — these are your hands and feet

### 03 Creator Matching Agent — finding creators, finally makes sense

Finding creators is a top headache for merchants. Either pricing is hard to negotiate, or you spend the money and the resulting video lands flat.

The problem isn't the creators — it's that the goal and the right fit weren't clear up front.

The Creator Matching Agent builds a tailored creator plan, sources candidates, negotiates details, and follows up on results. Closed-loop: you don't have to sit at the computer chasing people one at a time.

### 04 Competitor Analysis Agent — your "market intelligence officer"

Want to know which videos nearby stores have made viral lately? Which promos they're running? Which categories are trending?

Previously you had to scroll endlessly to find out.

Now the Competitor Analysis Agent tracks competitor marketing moves: hit content, market trends, competitor intel. Your operations follow the market instead of running blind.
